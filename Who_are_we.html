Who are we?
Its a Skills Developement Startup that is formulated by the vision of Founder and supported by her friends and like minded people. “Providing a platform to every youth of country to empower them by grooming their core hard skills as well Soft skills as per Industry”
Starting from Software Industry, we will take it across different industry.
Our Program:
It will be a virtual office (Live Online) that operates from 11:00 am -5:00 pm from Tuesday to Saturday. Yes Monday is off :)
What differentiate us:
1. No training sessions. Knowledge sessions wll be there where everyone can put his/her opinion and can understand the things deeply.
2. In every work assignment, your mentor/lead will guide you to do the job in more better way.
3. Every month , there is a meeting to evualte your performance as well performance of your mentor. You have give full rights to speak of.
