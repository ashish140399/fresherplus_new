Who are we?
Its a Skills Developement Startup that is formulated by the vision of Founder and supported by her friends and like minded people. “Providing a platform to every youth of country to empower them by grooming their core hard skills as well Soft skills as per Industry”
Starting from Software Industry, we will take it across different industry.
What differentiate us:
1. “Soldiers can be trained well only on real grounds”. After academics, students should be groomed in an actual professional environment by assigning and involving them on Live Projects. Responsibility makes a man better.
2. Returnship: We are providing a platform for experienced professionals (especially females) who wants to make a come back in the industry after the gap.
3. Momenurship: Our unique company policies will be a fortune for mothers who are passionate to work along with their kids. They can become our dedicated mentors and can become a partner in our venture as well.
4. No upfront fee for the students.
5. Last but not least, we are proud to follow our mission. We strongly hope that few of our groomed students will create a difference in their respective fields and make the nation proud.
